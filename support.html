FAQ

How do I use the Multiply ability?
Tap above the dotted line near the paddle when you have fewer than 6 balls active. The arrows and green cooldown bar indicate when it's ready.

What is Cleanup?
When 20 or fewer bricks remain, the ability switches to Cleanup mode. It launches targeted balls from the paddle directly at each remaining brick.

What is Party Mode?
When 40+ balls are active, Party Mode triggers — the borders go wild, a rainbow trampoline appears, and you can sit back and enjoy the show for 5 seconds.

How do I earn coins?
Destroy bricks, collect coin power-ups during gameplay, and complete achievements.
